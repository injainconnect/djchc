News & Updates

Stay connected with DJCHC community events and announcements

Latest News

Paryushana Parva Celebrations 2024

September 2024

DJCHC celebrated the 10-day Paryushana Mahaparva with great devotion. Special pravachans, Samayik sessions, and Pratikraman were organized daily. The community participated in large numbers with families coming together for this sacred festival.

Mahavir Jayanti Grand Celebration

April 2024

The birth anniversary of Lord Mahavir was celebrated with a grand Shobha Yatra (procession) in the Hi-Tec City area. Cultural programs by children, abhishek of Bhagwan Mahavir, and community bhojan were the highlights of the event.

Youth Wing — Pathshala Programs

Ongoing

Weekly Pathshala (religious education) classes are being conducted for children and youth every Sunday. The curriculum covers Jain philosophy, stories of Tirthankaras, Sanskrit shlokas, and moral values.

Health Camp & Blood Donation Drive

January 2024

DJCHC organized a free health check-up camp and blood donation drive as part of its social welfare initiatives. Over 100 community members participated in the blood donation drive.

Kshamavani Diwas — Day of Forgiveness

September 2024

The community gathered to seek and grant forgiveness on Kshamavani Diwas, marking the conclusion of Paryushana. "Micchami Dukkadam" echoed through the halls as members embraced the spirit of universal forgiveness.
