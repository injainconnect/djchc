About Us

Know more about DJCHC and our community

Who We Are

The Digambar Jain Centre of Hi-Tec City (DJCHC) was established by the Digambar Jain community members residing in and around Hi-Tec City, Madhapur, Kondapur, Gachibowli, and surrounding IT corridor areas of Hyderabad. As the IT industry brought a large influx of Jain professionals to this region, the need for a dedicated spiritual and community centre became evident.

Our History

With the rapid growth of the IT sector in Hyderabad's western corridor, many Digambar Jain families settled in the Hi-Tec City area. Recognizing the need for a local place of worship and community bonding, dedicated volunteers came together to establish DJCHC. What began as small gatherings in community halls has grown into a well-organized centre serving hundreds of families.

Our Values

Ahimsa (Non-Violence)

We uphold the supreme principle of non-violence in thought, word, and deed — the cornerstone of Jain philosophy.

Anekantavada (Non-Absolutism)

We embrace multiple perspectives and encourage open-minded thinking in our community discussions and decisions.

Aparigraha (Non-Possessiveness)

We practice simplicity and generosity, encouraging community service and charitable giving.

Seva (Service)

We believe in selfless service to the community through social welfare programs, education, and health initiatives.

What We Do

  • Daily Pooja, Aarti & Abhishek
  • Spiritual discourses (Pravachan) by Jain Muniraj and scholars
  • Paryushana, Mahavir Jayanti & Das Lakshan celebrations
  • Children's Pathshala (religious education)
  • Youth wing activities and cultural programs
  • Community meals and social gatherings
  • Health camps, blood donation drives & charitable initiatives

Community Affiliation

DJCHC is part of the broader Digambar Jain community network in Hyderabad and works in coordination with other Jain organizations in Telangana for religious, social, and charitable endeavors.
