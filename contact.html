Who We Are

The Digambar Jain Centre of Hi-Tec City (DJCHC) was established by the Digambar Jain community members residing in and around Hi-Tec City, Madhapur, Kondapur, Gachibowli, and surrounding IT corridor areas of Hyderabad. As the IT industry brought a large influx of Jain professionals to this region, the need for a dedicated spiritual and community centre became evident.
