Support the Digambar Jain community through your generous contributions

Contribute to Our Mission

Your donations help sustain daily temple rituals, community events, cultural programs, and maintenance of our two sacred temples — DJCHC (Hi-Tech City) and DJC Nallagandla.

Every contribution, big or small, strengthens our sangha and supports the preservation of Digambar Jain traditions in Hyderabad.

Donation Categories

Daily Pooja & Abhishek

Support daily worship, abhishek materials, flowers, and ritual essentials for both temples.

Chaturmaas Seva

Contribute to Chaturmaas arrangements — aahar, pravachan setup, and special event celebrations.

Temple Maintenance

Help maintain and improve temple infrastructure, cleanliness, and facilities.

Pathshala & Education

Fund Jain education programs, pathshala materials, and youth development activities.
