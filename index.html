About DJCHC

The Digambar Jain Centre of Hi-Tech City (DJCHC) is a spiritual and community hub located in the heart of Hyderabad's Hi-Tec City. It serves as a place of worship, cultural activities, and community gatherings for the Digambar Jain community in the region.

Our Mission

To promote the principles of Jainism — Ahimsa (non-violence), Anekantavada (non-absolutism), and Aparigraha (non-possessiveness) — while fostering a vibrant community for spiritual growth and cultural preservation.

Location

📍 Hi-Tec City, Hyderabad, Telangana, India
