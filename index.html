DJCHC, Hyderabad — A Spiritual & Community Hub

Welcome to DJCHC

The Digambar Jain Centre of Hi-Tec City (DJCHC) is a premier Jain temple and community centre located in the IT corridor of Hyderabad. Established to serve the growing Digambar Jain community in and around Hi-Tec City, Madhapur, and Gachibowli areas, DJCHC is a beacon of spirituality, culture, and service.

Our Mission

To promote the eternal principles of Jainism — Ahimsa (non-violence), Anekantavada (non-absolutism), and Aparigraha (non-possessiveness) — while building a vibrant community for spiritual growth, cultural preservation, and social welfare in the Hi-Tec City region of Hyderabad.

Activities & Services

Daily Pooja & Aarti

Regular morning and evening prayers conducted for the community.

Spiritual Discourses

Pravachans and Swadhyay sessions by learned Jain scholars and Muniraj.

Festival Celebrations

Grand celebrations of Paryushana, Mahavir Jayanti, Diwali, and other Jain festivals.

Community Gatherings

Social events, youth programs, and community meals bringing families together.
